Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

This clothing company takes a modern approach to sustainable and organic clothing. All the products are made from natural materials such as bamboo, hemp or organic cotton, with the goal of minimising our environmental footprint.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

To potential customers, the company is someone who cares deeply about the world around them, and feels that it is important to make ethical choices when producing consumer goods.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

This company would entice someone to purchase their products because consumers would know they are lowering their impact on the planet by making the choice to purchase clothing made from natural materials rather than synthetics.
