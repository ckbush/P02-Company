About

Canadian made and ethically accredited, Conscious Culture is more than just a clothing brand, but a community who believe clothing design should always be made with integrity.

We are passionate about creating simple, yet unique wardrobes for both men and women, at an affordable price. All of our clothes are made with ethical and sustainable materials such as tencel, hemp, bamboo, organic cotton, and recycled polyester, to name a few.

Starting as a small company, we now have a talented team of over 60 staff who work day and night to create the business we are today.
