About Us

Canadian made and ethically accredited, Conscious Culture is more than just a clothing brand, but a community of people who believe that clothing design should always be made with integrity.

We are passionate about creating simple, modern, and unique clothing items for both men and women, at an affordable price. All of our clothes are made with ethical and sustainable materials such as tencel, hemp, bamboo, organic cotton, and recycled polyester, to name a few.

Starting as a small company in April 2012, Conscious Culture now has a talented team of over 60 staff who work day and night to create the business we are today.
